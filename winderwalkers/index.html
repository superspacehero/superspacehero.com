Winder Walkers

Lead programmer, shader/environment artist

A Brackeys game jam entry starring a pair of wind-up toys

Winding Down

Moving consumes energy - conserve where you can by switching between your characters, and keep an eye out for wind-up stations.

Cooperation is Key

Your characters will have to work together to push buttons, pull levers, and wind up mechanisms to help each other progress.

A Little Pick-Me-Up

When more direct assistance is needed, you can approach your other character to share some of your energy, or pick them up to reach new heights.
